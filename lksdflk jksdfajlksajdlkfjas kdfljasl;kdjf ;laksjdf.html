ACADEMIC OVERVIEW

A Legacy of Excellence
in Education

Ananya Vidyalaya is a co-educational English Medium CBSE school committed to the highest standards of learning. We believe every child is unique — each student is nurtured and guided so their individual potential blossoms into true excellence, preparing them to be responsible citizens of India and the world.

Explore More

Our Story

Ananya Vidyalaya, located in the serene Zundal area of Ahmedabad, was founded with a vision to provide world-class education with a personal touch— nurturing every child in a warm and inspiring environment.

Our Vision

Promoting knowledge that enlightens by creatively enhancing learning processes and integrating tradition with modern educational technology to contribute towards creating a new world.

Our Mission

To facilitate an environment of learning where students and teachers develop skills of discipline, analytical and creative thinking, ethical and global mindset, and multilingual and collaborative approaches.
